Methodology

Data source, scope & how figures are computed

Pipeline status
Checking…
Automated pipeline checks the RBI portal daily at 11:00 AM IST and commits only when new data is published.
Data source & scope Sourced from the Reserve Bank of India's monthly bank-wise ATM/POS/Card Statistics portal. This dashboard covers the latest 12 published months and shows Credit Card figures only — Debit Card columns in the source file are intentionally excluded from every metric here. Figures are bank-reported to RBI and republished as-is; this tool does not adjust, estimate, or restate them.
How figures are computed
  • Market Share % — bank's outstanding cards ÷ total outstanding cards in the current filtered scope.
  • Net Additions — this month's outstanding cards minus the same bank's prior reporting month.
  • Industry Spend — sum of POS + Online/e-com + Others transaction value (₹ Cr) on credit cards; excludes ATM cash withdrawal value.
  • Avg Spend / Card — Industry Spend ÷ Outstanding Cards.
  • Avg Ticket Size — Industry Spend ÷ total transaction volume.
  • MoM / YoY % — percentage change vs. the prior reporting month / same month prior year, where that month's data is available.
  • HHI — Herfindahl-Hirschman Index, the sum of squared market shares (0–10,000 scale); >2,500 is conventionally "highly concentrated."
Verify a number yourself Every figure here traces back to a public RBI spreadsheet — nothing is behind a paywall or a private dataset. Cross-check any month directly on the RBI ATM/POS/Card Statistics portal.
Spotted a discrepancy? If a number here doesn't match the source RBI file, please flag it — this tool is actively maintained. Report a discrepancy
